HRC CORE VALUES

Core values are the things that drive your behaviour. The foundation for our thoughts and emotions, approach and perspective to life.
1. FAITH – The practical expressions of our Confidence in God and His Word.
2. RIGHTEOUSNESS – As a gift – Having a right standing with God and as a lifestyle – standard of behaviour according to the Bible.
3. EXCELLENCE – HRC is the embassy of God on earth. Every embassy is the reflection of the home country. The achievement of good quality in an unusual way.
4. LEADERSHIP – Taking the Lead, blazing the trail, Setting the pace; Light of the World

5. LOVE – HRC exist because of compassion for people.
6. ACCOUNTABILITY – We value and respect Authority.
7. RESPONSIBILITY – There is a sense of purpose and destiny in HRC family
