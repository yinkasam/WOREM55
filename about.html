Where it all began

HOW HRC STARTED
I was an undergraduate, 300 level student studying Economics in the University. As a Christian, I love the Lord and I wanted to know his assignment for my life. So, I bought a new book, titled “PURPOSE DRIVEN LIFE” written by Rick Warren. I had a very strong desire to understand God’s Purpose and Plan for my life. So, I decided to go on a 40 days fasting and prayer as prescribed by that book. Then on the 13th day of my fasting which was on 1st May, 2008, the Vision and my Word came in Isaiah 61:1-3. Then I knew I was called by God to be a Deliverer and a Restorer of Destinies. Also, the Name, Word of Deliverance and Revival Evangelical Mission (WODREM) was given to me Jan. 2009 from Psalm 107:20. And from the mandate, the Church’s name came later as House of Restoration Church worldwide. Initially, House of Restoration Assembly.

Through the knowledge gathered at WOFBI (BCC - 2008 & LCC - 2009 at Living Faith Church Worldwide), (WINNERS’ CHAPEL), I knew there was need for me to serve under an existing ministry for training and service which was for about 7 years (2009-2016). After which, HRC worldwide Inaugural service was held on Saturday 2nd July, 2016 by Apostle Blessing Salami, the General Overseer of Kingdom Harvest Gospel Ministry, Ilorin where I had served. And the following day, on Sunday 3rd July, 2016, in a place with about 40 seat capacity, which was former used as a shop at 4, Ejiba street off stadium road Ilorin, the first Sunday service was held there with 8 members in total attendance.
