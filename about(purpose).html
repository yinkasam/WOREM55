HRC GENERAL STATEMENT OF PURPOSE

Why do we Exist?
HRC is not a club. HRC is a cult. It’s a Church owned by Jesus Christ. It is not an entertainment centre. It exists for a serious divine business. It is about destiny. It is about life. It is not just about an individual, or our nation and generation but for the generations after us, if Jesus tarries. It is about eternity and God’s plan for mankind. When you talk about purpose, the reason for a thing. (Rom. 8:28; Prov. 19:21) The purpose of this church is being established and there is no force that can stop this vision and the divine purpose of this church. HRC is NOT a man-made idea. It is a divine idea for the end time harvest of souls into the Kingdom. So it can’t be stopped by any man or any force of darkness. Amen! There are five purposes established in the scripture for the universal church as reflected in Matt. 22:37-40; Matt. 28:11-20; Acts 2:42-47

1. FELLOWSHIP – It is the love and care that we share among the members of the church
2. EVANGELISM – Our assignment of winning people over from the kingdom of the devil into the Kingdom of God
3. WORSHIP – It is our Intimate relationship with God and the expression of our love for Him
4. MINISTRY –Our Service for God and humanity
5. DISCIPLESHIP – It is the process of maturing or growing Christians up into the image of Christ.
