BENEFITS OF HRC MEMBERSHIP

It is not enough to believe; you must belong to a church family like HRC. Anyone that says, “I don’t need a church” is either ignorant or arrogant or both. Member means a part of the body. So, we don’t just want you to be an attender of the body of Christ, you need to be a member of the body of Christ through a local assembly.

1. You will be identified as a genuine Christian
2. You are a part of a spiritual family that supports and encourages you (Eph. 4:16)
3. You have a place to discover, develop and deploy your gifts. You will have opportunity to serve in the church. Your gift will be discovered.

4. You are under the protection of godly leaders. Spiritual protection against wicked spirits and forces of darkness.
5. You are accountable to others in the church through your pastors or team leader. And this helps you to grow spiritually. You will enjoy correction that prevents you from destruction.
6. Be a member makes you an active player in the body not just an attender who is a spectator.
7. You will enjoy the best blessing from the Lord because you are a member. A member is a contributor while an attender is only a consumer. We are designed for connection and not for isolation. Responsibility is the key to greatness. Be a member not just an attender.
