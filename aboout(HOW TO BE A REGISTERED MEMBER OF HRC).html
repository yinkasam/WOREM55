HOW TO BE A REGISTERED MEMBER OF HRC

1. Personal profession Christ – you must be born again
2. Do water baptism by immersion
3. Completion of Membership School
4. Signing or taking of membership pledge

MEMBERSHIP PLEDGE 1. I will protect the unity of my church by: (i) Following the leaders, (ii) Acting in love towards other members and refusing to gossip because gossip kills a church

2. I will share the responsibilities of my church by: (i) Praying for its growth of my church (ii) Inviting unsaved people to attend church programs (iii) Give warm welcome to those visiting our church

3. I will serve the ministry of my church by: (i) Developing a servant –heart (ii) Discovering and developing my gifts and talents and deploying them for service in my church (iii) Submitting to continuous training by my Pastor

4. I will support and uphold the testimony of my church: (i) by attending service faithfully, (ii) living a godly Christian life, and (iii) paying my tithes and other contributions regularly.
